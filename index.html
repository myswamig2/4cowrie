The Three IoWLH Pillars

1

Know Thyself

Before a reading can be trusted, the reader and seeker must become aware of self. This includes motives, emotional state, fear, desire, memory, projection, and expectation.

Self-knowledge is the first purification of perception. Without it, divination may be bent toward preference rather than truth.

2

Cleanse and Purify

Purification is the work of removing what clouds the channel. Agitation, confusion, resentment, impurity of intent, and spiritual heaviness all interfere with accurate interpretation.

To cleanse and purify is not to become perfect. It is to become properly prepared.

3

Living in Alignment with Absolute Truth

The highest aim of divination is not curiosity. It is alignment. The seeker must be willing to obey, adjust, and live according to what is revealed.

Truth has little value when it is heard but not lived.

The Three Fundamental Responses

The image identifies three basic divinatory responses: Yes, No, and Definitely. In this module, these are understood not only as answers, but as spiritual states linked to the IoWLH pillars.

Yes

Opening, recognition, awareness

→

No

Warning, restraint, correction

→

Definitely

Certainty, stability, alignment

Yes → Know Thyself “Yes” is the opening of consciousness. It suggests recognition, awareness, and the first moment of truthful seeing.
No → Cleanse and Purify “No” often teaches more than denial. It points to what must be corrected, refined, or cleared before movement.
Definitely → Alignment with Absolute Truth “Definitely” is firm confirmation. It reflects grounded truth, balance, and clear agreement with divine order.

The Five Possibilities

From the three basic responses, the divinatory field expands into five possibilities associated with the shell pattern. These provide nuance, movement, and interpretive depth.

4 Open

Alafia

Peace, blessing, openness, favorable movement. Often expresses a strong yes, though sometimes still requiring context.

3 Open

Etawa

Qualified yes. Openness with condition. Progress is possible, but awareness and refinement are still needed.

2 Open

Ejife

Balance, agreement, stability, centered truth. Often read as strong confirmation and reliable support.

1 Open

Okana

Warning, uncertainty, resistance, delay, reconsideration. The matter is not yet clear or ready.

0 Open

Oyeku

Closure, denial, inwardness, deep no. Sometimes a withdrawal, sometimes a summons to introspection.

Reading the Five Possibilities Through the Pillars

Know Thyself

Most strongly reflected in Alafia as opening and recognition, and at times in Oyeku when the reading requires honest inward examination.

Cleanse and Purify

Strongly reflected in Etawa, Okana, and sometimes Oyeku, where refinement, caution, or spiritual clearing is needed.

Alignment with Absolute Truth

Most clearly reflected in Ejife, where balance, certainty, agreement, and stable truth are present.

Discussion Questions

  1. Why must self-knowledge come before interpretation?
  2. How does purification change the quality of a reading?
  3. Why is “Definitely” different from a simple “Yes”?
  4. Which of the five possibilities best expresses stable truth, and why?
  5. How can a divinatory “No” be understood as guidance rather than rejection?

Practice Reflection and Assignment

Reflection Practice

Take one real question from your life. Sit quietly and reflect through the three pillars before seeking an answer.

  1. Know Thyself: What bias, desire, fear, or expectation might be influencing your interpretation?
  2. Cleanse and Purify: What in your thought, emotion, conduct, or environment needs to be cleared?
  3. Living in Alignment with Absolute Truth: What truth must you be willing to accept and obey?

Written Assignment

Write a short response titled: “How the Three Pillars Prepare a Person for Divination.”

  1. Define each pillar in your own words.
  2. Explain how each pillar relates to Yes, No, and Definitely.
  3. Briefly describe the five possibilities and their spiritual value.
  4. Conclude with one paragraph on how divination helps shape reality through truth and alignment.

Closing Insight

Divination does not begin with shells alone. It begins with the condition of the person who approaches the oracle.

To Know Thyself is to become honest.
To Cleanse and Purify is to become clear.
To Live in Alignment with Absolute Truth is to become ready.

Then the answers can be heard. Then the possibilities can be weighed. Then Odu can be received not merely as information, but as wisdom.
