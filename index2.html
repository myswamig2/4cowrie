In this lesson, the pillars are understood as inner states that make correct divination possible. One must know oneself, remove distortion, and be prepared to live according to truth. Without this preparation, a reading may be heard, but not properly understood.

The Three IoWLH Pillars

1

Know Thyself

The first pillar demands self-awareness. The seeker and diviner must recognize bias, fear, desire, projection, and emotional coloring.

Self-knowledge is the beginning of clear perception. It keeps the reading from becoming an echo of personal preference.

2

Cleanse and Purify

The second pillar addresses preparation. Agitation, confusion, resentment, and impure intention can cloud the divinatory channel.

Purification is the disciplined clearing of what interferes with truth.

3

Living in Alignment with Absolute Truth

The third pillar calls for integrity. Divination is not only about receiving an answer. It is about living according to what is revealed.

Truth becomes transformative only when it is accepted and embodied.

The Three Fundamental Responses

The image identifies three broad divinatory responses: Yes, No, and Definitely. In this module, each response is tied to a pillar.

Yes

Opening, recognition, awareness

→

No

Warning, restraint, correction

→

Definitely

Certainty, stability, alignment

Yes → Know Thyself

“Yes” marks the opening of awareness. It often signals recognition, receptivity, and the first truthful seeing of a matter.

No → Cleanse and Purify

“No” is not merely denial. It often points to what must be corrected, cleared, restrained, or refined before the matter can proceed.

Definitely → Absolute Truth

“Definitely” represents grounded confirmation. It signals a stronger and more stable alignment with what is real and spiritually sound.

The Five Possibilities

From the three broad responses, interpretation expands through the five shell possibilities. These provide gradation, balance, and nuance.

4 Open

Alafia

Peace, openness, blessing, favorable movement. Often read as a strong yes, though sometimes still needing context.

3 Open

Etawa

Qualified yes. A path may be open, but conditions, attentiveness, or refinement remain necessary.

2 Open

Ejife

Balance, agreement, stability, centered truth. Often the clearest expression of reliable confirmation.

1 Open

Okana

Warning, resistance, uncertainty, caution. The matter may require pause, correction, or recasting.

0 Open

Oyeku

Closure, inwardness, denial, deep stillness. Sometimes a refusal, sometimes a call to deeper introspection.

The Five Possibilities Through the Pillars

Know Thyself

Reflected strongly in Alafia as opening and awareness, and at times in Oyeku when the reading drives the seeker inward toward honest self-examination.

Cleanse and Purify

Reflected strongly in Etawa, Okana, and sometimes Oyeku, where readiness is incomplete and refinement is needed.

Alignment with Absolute Truth

Most clearly expressed through Ejife, where balance, certainty, and stable agreement are present.

From Possibility to Odu

The five possibilities are not the final destination. They are the threshold. Once the condition of the matter is sensed, interpretation deepens through the body of Odu.

Key Teaching Statement

The Three Pillars of Divination are not merely concepts. They are the inner disciplines that prepare the seeker to hear truth correctly. To know oneself is to recognize distortion. To cleanse and purify is to remove obstruction. To live in alignment with absolute truth is to become ready for authentic guidance.

Discussion Questions

  1. Why must self-knowledge come before interpretation?
  2. How does purification improve the quality of divination?
  3. Why is “Definitely” stronger than a simple “Yes”?
  4. Which possibility most clearly reflects balance and truth?
  5. How can a divinatory “No” function as guidance rather than rejection?

Practice Assignment

Write a short response titled: “How the Three Pillars Prepare a Person for Divination.”

  1. Define each pillar in your own words.
  2. Explain how each pillar relates to Yes, No, and Definitely.
  3. Describe the five possibilities briefly.
  4. Conclude with a paragraph on how truth shapes reality through divination.

Cleanse and Purify so that the channel is clear.
